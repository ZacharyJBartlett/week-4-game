Ben Gates

Your mission, should you choose to accept it, is to steal the Declaration of Independance.

By clicking on Nicholas Cage's face, you will begin picking the lock to the Declaration of Independance's case.

You win the game by unlocking the case, you lose by overshooting and alerting the police.

The value of each Cage face is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each face.
